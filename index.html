DVOS (Digital Visibility OS) is a research visibility platform that helps academics build their online presence. It tracks your citations in real time, verifies your ORCID profile, generates an AI-powered researcher bio, surfaces grant opportunities and conferences, and shows exactly how discoverable your work is to funders and collaborators. A free account gives you core tools forever; the Researcher plan unlocks full AI capabilities.
DVOS is freemium. Every new account gets a 14-day free trial with full access to all features — no credit card required. After the trial, the Free plan keeps your visibility score, ORCID tracking, and basic arXiv feed forever at no cost. The Researcher plan ($9/month or $79/year) unlocks the AI Profile Builder, AI Content Engine, full Research Pulse (grants + conferences), Collaboration Cards, and Journal Check. Institutions can contact us for custom multi-seat pricing.
When your 14-day trial ends, your account automatically moves to the Free plan. You keep your visibility score, ORCID verification, and basic research feed forever. You can upgrade any time from inside the app.
You can sign in with your email and password, receive a magic link (no password needed), or use Google, Apple, or ORCID single sign-on. ORCID sign-in automatically links your verified researcher identity to your DVOS profile. Signing up takes under 30 seconds — just click "Start free trial" above.

All citation data is free and updates in real time.

Grants from Wellcome Trust, NIH Fogarty, Gates Foundation, African Union, and NRF South Africa are surfaced in the platform.
DVOS computes a score from 0–100 across six signals: ORCID completeness (25%), citation impact (27%), researcher profile (18%), research website (12%), content output (10%), and platform presence (8%). The score reflects how discoverable your work is to collaborators, funders, and journals. It updates every time you improve a signal.
ORCID is a free, persistent identifier for researchers — like a passport for your academic work. It solves name disambiguation, ensures all your publications are attributed to you across databases, and is required for most international grant applications including EU Horizon and Wellcome Trust. DVOS can sign you in directly with ORCID, automatically populating your profile.
