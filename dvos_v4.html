Predatory journal warning signs
Know before you submit
🚩 Unsolicited email invitesLegitimate journals rarely cold-email researchers to submit.
🚩 Very rapid peer reviewAccepted within days of submission — review quality is absent.
🚩 Fake impact metrics"Global Impact Factor" or "Index Copernicus Value" are not the Clarivate JIF.
🚩 Not in DOAJ or ScopusAbsence from DOAJ, Scopus, or Web of Science is a strong warning signal.
🚩 High APC, no waiverCharging substantial APCs with no waiver policy for low-income countries is a red flag.
🚩 Misleading journal nameNames mimicking prestigious journals need careful verification — check ISSN on DOAJ.
